Selection

Each team consists of 11 players (no substitutes) with the following restrictions:

  • 1 Goalkeeper (GK)
  • 3 to 5 Defenders (DF)
  • 3 to 5 Midfielders (MF)
  • 1 to 3 Forwards (FW)

Draft

The draft will take place from Tuesday 15th November 2022 to Saturday 19th November 2022 inclusive. On each round every manager will choose a single player to join their team. Each manager should do their scouting in advance, so 1 or 2 rounds can be completed each day. The order will be randomly generated and will alternate on each round. For example, if there are 3 teams denoted A, B, C, the order will be: A-B-C-C-B-A ... The draft is completed as soon as all teams have 11 players. At the end of the draft all teams must comply with the selection restrictions.

Transfers

A manager can exchange one of their players for an unselected player on a first-come-first-served basis. There are no restrictions on the number of transfers allowed. Teams must comply with the selection restrictions at all times. Transfers can be submitted at any time after the draft is completed, and are immediately binding. However, ownership for the purpose of assigning points will only be transferred at the following times:

  • 22:30 GMT, Saturday 19th November 2022 - Night before Qatar v Ecuador
  • 22:30 GMT, Thursday 24th November 2022 - Night before Wales v Iran
  • 22:30 GMT, Monday 28th November 2022 - Night before Netherlands v Qatar
  • 22:30 GMT, Friday 2nd December 2022 - Night before first knockout match
  • 22:30 GMT, Thursday 8th December 2022 - Night before first quarter-final
  • 22:30 GMT, Monday 12th December 2022 - Night before first semi-final
  • 22:30 GMT, Saturday 17th December 2022 - Night before final

Points

Points are awarded to or deducted from players for the following events. These points are assigned to the team owning the player at the time of the event. Positions are determined by the list of players, not apparent playing position in a given match. FIFA World Cup 2022 official tournament reports are used to calculate points in all cases. No points are awarded or deducted for any events occurring during the Third place play-off.
