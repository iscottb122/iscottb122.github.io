Selection

Each team consists of 11 players (no substitutes) with the following restrictions:

  • 1 Goalkeeper (GK)
  • 3 to 5 Defenders (DF)
  • 3 to 5 Midfielders (MF)
  • 1 to 3 Forwards (FW)

Draft

The draft will take place from Thursday 3rd June 2021 to Thursday 10th June 2021 inclusive. On each round every manager will choose a single player to join their team. Each manager should do their scouting in advance, so 1 or 2 rounds can be completed each day. The order will be randomly generated and will alternate on each round. For example, if there are 3 teams denoted A, B, C, the order will be: A-B-C-C-B-A ... The draft is completed as soon as all teams have 11 players. At the end of the draft all teams must comply with the selection restrictions.

Transfers

A manager can exchange one of their players for an unselected player on a first-come-first-served basis. There are no restrictions on the number of transfers allowed. Teams must comply with the selection restrictions at all times. Transfers can be submitted at any time after the draft is completed, and are immediately binding. However, ownership for the purpose of assigning points will only be transferred at the following times:

  • 00:01 BST, Friday 11th June 2021 - Morning of Turkey v Italy
  • 00:01 BST, Wednesday 16th June 2021 - Morning of Turkey v Wales
  • 00:01 BST, Sunday 20th June 2021 - Morning of Switzerland v Turkey
  • 00:01 BST, Saturday 26th June 2021 - Morning of first knockout match
  • 00:01 BST, Friday 2nd July 2021 - Morning of first quarter-final
  • 00:01 BST, Tuesday 6th July 2021 - Morning of first semi-final
  • 00:01 BST, Sunday 11th July 2021 - Morning of final

Points

Points are awarded to or deducted from players for the following events. These points are assigned to the team owning the player at the time of the event. Positions are determined by the list of players, not apparent playing position in a given match. UEFA Euro 2020 official tournament reports are used to calculate points in all cases.
